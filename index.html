About the Collective

Pioneer Indians is an Eggenfelden-based cultural association that curates joyful encounters between Indian diasporic communities and local Bavarian neighbours.

Our volunteer teams organize pop-up food markets, storytelling salons, film screenings, and language tandems that highlight shared values of hospitality and creativity.

Intercultural Dialogues

Monthly salons invite artists, researchers, and neighbours to co-create unforgettable evenings of exchange.

Creative Learning

Hands-on workshops explore rangoli design, Bollywood choreography, regional cuisines, and mindful storytelling.

Community Support

We host newcomer welcome circles, mentorship sessions, and resource exchanges for families settling into Lower Bavaria.
